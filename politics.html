A year away, Zeldin is in the lead

By Laura Heard, Briana Ellis-Gibbs & Jacob Schermerhorn

Lee Zeldin is a member of Congress whose campaign contributions have far surpassed those of his opponents. Zeldin has gotten 3.28 million dollars from individual contributions, while his opponents have only gotten an average of 440.41K in contributions from individual donors. Rob Anstorio got 663.69K, Andrew Giuliani got 394.69K, and 262.82K in contributions from individuals. When it comes to corporation donation, Zeldin got 134.2K contributions while Hochul has the second-largest amount of corporate donations with 39.25K dollars, Giuliani got 8.48K in donations, and Astorino got 17K. He got the most from a PLLC/LLC with a total donation of 92.66K, and Astorino got the second-largest donation of 48.7K. Zeldin was also the only candidate to receive money for a union. He got 5K in contributions from a union.
