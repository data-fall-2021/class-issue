Don’t Expect Gas Heating and Stoves in New York City’s new buildings

2024 and 2027 the ban goes into effect for small and large buildings respectively

By Demetria Osei-Tutu, Tatiana Krisztina & Izania Gonzalez

Fossil fuels are the biggest source of greenhouse gas emissions. The New York City Council voted to pass a law on Wednesday to change that. The law is set to stop building developers from installing and renovating fuel burning system starting in 2024. Heating and hot water systems are the source of about 42% of NYC's greenhouse gas emissions.
